Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Spaghetti Bolognese is a rich Italian pasta dish made with slow-cooked minced beef, tomatoes, onions, and herbs. It is hearty, flavorful, and perfect for a family meal.

This recipe is simple but tastes like it has been cooked for hours. The meat sauce coats the spaghetti beautifully, creating a comforting and delicious dish.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 250g minced beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté onions, garlic, and carrot.
  3. Add minced beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  5. Let the sauce simmer for 15–20 minutes.
  6. Serve the sauce over the cooked spaghetti.
  7. Enjoy your delicious Spaghetti Bolognese!
